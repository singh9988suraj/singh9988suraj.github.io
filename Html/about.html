About us

what makes us special?

The Food Foundation of India is a secular, not-for-profit organization in India, registered under the Societies’ Registration Act of 1860. Set up in 2015, the organization works for care and feed feed poor people to improve their quality of life’.

The Food Foundation of India envisions a society where all have the right get food. It recently became the first and only Indian organization to be honoured with the National Award 2020’ for its exemplary work in the field of ageing, relief efforts work during the Covid 19 pandemic
